Hyperglycemia
protocolid=24
===================
EMTP/I
---------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Assess for signs of trauma. Provide spinal immobilization as necessary.
<tr><td>4.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>5.<td>For altered mental status, perform rapid glucose determination.
<tr><td>6.<td>If glucose greater than 300 mg / dL, start an IV of normal saline.
<tr><td>7.<td>For signs and symptoms of hypovolemic shock or dehydration, follow the <i><u>Shock protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Place on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret <i><u>12 lead ECG</u></i> as indicated.
<tr><td>9.<td>Transport and perform ongoing assessment as indicated.
