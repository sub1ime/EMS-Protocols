EMTB
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Assess mechanism of injury and / or nature of illness.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer Oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>If shock is present, without pulsating masses, refer to Shock protocol.
<tr><td>5.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret 12 lead ECG.
<tr><td>6.<td>Transport promptly in position of comfort. Reassess as needed.

EMTEN
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Assess mechanism of injury and / or nature of illness.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer Oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>If shock is present, without pulsating masses, refer to Shock protocol.
<tr><td>5.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret 12 lead ECG.
<tr><td>6.<td>Initiate IV of Normal Saline KVO. Establish second IV if time permits.
<tr><td>7.<td>Transport promptly in position of comfort. Reassess as needed.

EMTI
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Assess mechanism of injury and / or nature of illness.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer Oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>If shock is present, without pulsating masses, refer to Shock protocol.
<tr><td>5.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret 12 lead ECG.
<tr><td>6.<td>Initiate IV of Normal Saline KVO. Establish second IV if time permits.
<tr><td>7.<td>Administer Normal Saline 20 mL / kg bolus twice. Caution should be used in patients with a history of renal failure and HF. Reassess for overload.
<tr><td>8.<td>If patient has not responded to boluses, contact medical control to consider the administration of DOPAMINE 5 - 20 mcg / kg / min for hypotension that remains after fluid bolus. Titrate to maintain adequate peripheral perfusion.
<tr><td>9.<td>Transport promptly in position of comfort. Reassess as needed.
