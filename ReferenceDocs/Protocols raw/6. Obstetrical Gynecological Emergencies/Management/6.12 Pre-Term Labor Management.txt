Pre-Term Labor

=====================
1. Perform general patient management.
2. Support life-threatening problems associated with
airway, breathing, and circulation.
3. Administer oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
4. Place patient in left lateral recumbent position.
5. Determine if patient is in labor and monitor frequency,
intensity, and durations of contractions.
6. Prepare for delivery.
7. Establish an IV of Normal Saline if clinically indicated.
8. If the patient is exhibiting symptoms of shock, refer to
the Shock protocol.
9. Transport promptly and reassess as indicated.
