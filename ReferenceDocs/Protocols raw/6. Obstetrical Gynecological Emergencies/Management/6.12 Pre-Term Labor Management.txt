Pre-Term Labor
protocolid=61
=====================
EMTP/EMTI/EMTEN
------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>Place patient in left lateral recumbent position.
<tr><td>5.<td>Determine if patient is in labor and monitor frequency, intensity, and durations of contractions.
<tr><td>6.<td>Prepare for delivery.
<tr><td>7.<td>Establish an IV of Normal Saline if clinically indicated.
<tr><td>8.<td>If the patient is exhibiting symptoms of shock, refer to the <i><u>Shock protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>9.<td>Transport promptly and reassess as indicated.
