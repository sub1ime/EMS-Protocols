Post-Partum Hemorrhage
protocolid=62
=================
EMTP/EMTI
------------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>If atonic uterus is noted, firmly massage fundus.
<tr><td>5.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor.
<tr><td>6.<td>Establish an IV of Normal Saline. Establish a second IV if clinically indicated. Do not delay transport to start a second IV.
<tr><td>7.<td>If the patient is exhibiting symptoms of shock, refer to the <i><u>Shock protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Transport promptly and reassess as indicated.
