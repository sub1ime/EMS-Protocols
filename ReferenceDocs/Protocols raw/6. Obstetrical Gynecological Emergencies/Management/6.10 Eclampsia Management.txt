Eclampsia

======================
1. Perform general patient management.
2. Support life-threatening problems associated with
airway, breathing, and circulation. Suction the
oropharynx if needed.
3. Administer oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
4. Obtain blood glucose sample. If < 60 mg / dL or > 300
mg / dL refer to Hypoglycemia or Hyperglycemia
protocol.
5. Establish an IV of Normal Saline. If signs of shock are
present, refer to Shock protocol.

6. If eclampsia is noted (characterized by seizures,
hypertension, and / or coma), administer bolus of
MAGNESIUM SULFATE 4 Gm IV over 5 - 10 minutes.
7. If seizure persists, administer MIDAZOLAM 0.1 mg / kg
IV / IM (max single dose 5 mg). If midazolam is
unavailable, give DIAZEPAM 0.25 mg / kg up to 5 mg
slow IV.
8. Transport promptly to an appropriate facility with
obstetrical services and reassess as indicated.
