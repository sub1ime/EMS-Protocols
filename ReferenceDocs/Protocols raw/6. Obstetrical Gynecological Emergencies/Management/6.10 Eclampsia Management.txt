Eclampsia
protoclid=59
======================
EMTP/EMTI
-------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation. Suction the oropharynx if needed.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>Obtain blood glucose sample. If < 60 mg / dL or > 300 mg / dL refer to <i><u>Hypoglycemia</u></i> or <i><u>Hyperglycemia protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>5.<td>Establish an IV of Normal Saline. If signs of shock are present, refer to <i><u>Shock protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>6.<td>If eclampsia is noted (characterized by seizures, hypertension, and / or coma), administer bolus of <i><u>MAGNESIUM SULFATE</u></i> 4 Gm IV over 5 - 10 minutes.
<tr><td>7.<td>If seizure persists, administer <i><u>MIDAZOLAM</u></i> 0.1 mg / kg IV / IM (max single dose 5 mg). If midazolam is unavailable, give <i><u>DIAZEPAM</u></i> 0.25 mg / kg up to 5 mg slow IV.
<tr><td>8.<td>Transport promptly to an appropriate facility with obstetrical services and reassess as indicated.
