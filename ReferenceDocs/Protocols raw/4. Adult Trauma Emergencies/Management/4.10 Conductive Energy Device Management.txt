4.10 Conductive Energy Device Mangement

==================================
1. Perform general patient assessment.
2. Administer oxygen, to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%. Support
respirations as necessary with a BVM.
3. Determine history of events between the time the
weapon was used until EMS arrived.
4. Per patient assessment (chest pain / palpitations) place
patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret 12 lead
ECG. Refer to appropriate Cardiac Care protocol.
5. Establish IV of Normal Saline at KVO rate, per
assessment.
6. If patient is agitated or combative, refer to Behavioral
Emergencies protocol.
7. Transport promptly in position of comfort and reassess
as needed.
