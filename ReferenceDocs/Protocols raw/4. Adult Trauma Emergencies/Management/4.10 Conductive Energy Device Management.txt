4.10 Conductive Energy Device Mangement
protocolid=43
==================================
EMTP/EMTI
-------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient assessment.
<tr><td>2.<td>Administer oxygen, to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Support respirations as necessary with a BVM.
<tr><td>3.<td>Determine history of events between the time the weapon was used until EMS arrived.
<tr><td>4.<td>Per patient assessment (chest pain / palpitations) place patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret <i><u>12 lead ECG</u></i>. Refer to appropriate <i><u>Cardiac Care protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>5.<td>Establish IV of Normal Saline at KVO rate, per assessment.
<tr><td>6.<td>If patient is agitated or combative, refer to <i><u>Behavioral Emergencies protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>7.<td>Transport promptly in position of comfort and reassess as needed.
