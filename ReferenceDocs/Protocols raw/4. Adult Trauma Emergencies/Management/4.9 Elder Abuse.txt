Elder Abuse
protocolid=42
==========================
EMTP/EMTI/EMTEN/EMTB
-----------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems; C-spine precautions.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen, to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Support respirations as necessary with a BVM.
<tr><td>4.<td>Observe and record objectively the surroundings and conditions of the scene and patient.
<tr><td>5.<td>Refer to the appropriate <i><u>Medical</u></i> or <i><u>Trauma</u></i> Patient Care protocol for obvious injuries / illnesses.
<tr><td>6.<td>UNDER VIRGINIA LAW, EMS PROVIDERS ARE MANDATORY REPORTERS OF SUSPECTED ELDER ABUSE. (<i><u>VA code 63.2-1606</u></i>) Report suspicions to the receiving facility emergency department attending physician on arrival, or report suspicions immediately to Adult Protective Services. <b>APS Hotline: 888-83-ADULT (888-832-3858)</b>.
<tr><td>7.<td>Transport as soon as possible.
