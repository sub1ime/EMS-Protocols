Sexual Assault
protocolid=41
========================
EMTP/EMTI/EMTEN/EMTB
----------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Obtain general patient assessment.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Assess for signs of trauma; take C-spine precautions per assessment.
<tr><td>4.<td>Administer Oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>5.<td>Perform wound care only to assess the severity and provide hemorrhage control.
<tr><td>6.<td>If an acute medical condition is noted, refer to appropriate <i><u>Medical Patient protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>7.<td>If physical trauma is noted, refer to appropriate <i><u>Trauma Patient protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Discourage the patient from changing clothes or bathing.
<tr><td>9.<td>Transport promptly in position of comfort to appropriate facility. Reassess as needed.
