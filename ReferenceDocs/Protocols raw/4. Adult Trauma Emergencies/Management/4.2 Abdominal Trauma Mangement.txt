EMTP/EMTI
--------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Maintain scene and provider safety.
<tr><td>2.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer supplemental oxygen to maintain a <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. If needed assist ventilations with BVM but avoid hyperventilation, maintain C-spine precautions.
<tr><td>4.<td>Identify mechanism of injury.
<tr><td>5.<td>Establish large bore IV’s of normal saline. Titrate to systolic blood pressure of 90 to 100 mmHg.
<tr><td>6.<td>Treat pain if indicated. Refer to <i><u>pain management protocol.
<tr><td>7.<td> Consider <i><u>ONDANSETRON (ZOFRAN)</u></i> 0.1 mg / kg slow IVP over 2 - 5 minutes, max 4.0 mg per dose as needed per <i><u>Nausea and vomiting</u></i> protocol. Do not give PO meds.
<tr><td>8.<td>Transport to the appropriate hospital per <i><u>Trauma Triage Scheme</u></i>.
<tr><td>9.<td>Reassess patient as indicated.
