Electrical Injuries
protocolid=38
=====================================
EMTP/EMTI
----------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management. Do not touch the patient if they are in contact with the electrical source.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Consider supporting respirations with a BVM.
<tr><td>4.<td>Determine extent of any burn injuries. Refer to the <i><u>Burns protocol</u></i>. Avoid initiating IVs in areas of burn unless absolutely necessary.
<tr><td>5.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor; obtain/interpret <i><u>12 Lead ECG</u></i>. Refer to the appropriate <i><u>Cardiac Care protocol</u></i> for dysrhythmias. If hyperkalemia is suspected, contact Medical Control.
<tr><td>6.<td>Establish an IV of normal saline at KVO.
<tr><td>7.<td>Consider administration of pain management per <i><u>Pain Management protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Transport to a trauma facility and perform ongoing assessment as indicated.
