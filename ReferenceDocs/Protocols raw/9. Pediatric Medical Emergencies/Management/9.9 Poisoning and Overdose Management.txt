protocolid=82

EMTP/EMTI
---------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>Establish an IV of normal saline per patient assessment.
<tr><td>5.<td>If respiratory effort remains diminished and opiate administration is suspected, give <i><u>NARCAN</u></i> 0.1 mg / kg slow IVP max 2mg.
<tr><td>6.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor and monitor pulse oximetry.
<tr><td>7.<td>Transport and perform ongoing assessment as indicated.
