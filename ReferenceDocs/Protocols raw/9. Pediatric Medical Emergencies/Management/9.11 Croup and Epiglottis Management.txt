Croup and Epiglottis
protocolid=84
===========================
EMTP/EMTI/EMTEN
--------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Support respirations as necessary with a BVM.
<tr><td>4.<td>Place patient in a position of comfort, typically sitting upright.
<tr><td>5.<td>May administer a Normal Saline nebulizer (5 mL).
<tr><td>6.<td><i><u>ALBUTEROL</u></i> nebulizer may be given.
<tr><td>7.<td>Transport in position of comfort.
