If the patient is having a seizure, refer to the Pediatric

If temperature is greater than 106° F / 41°C, refer to
Hyperthermia Patient Care Protocol.
Begin passive cooling by removing excess and
constrictive clothing. Avoid over-exposure.
Obtain blood glucose sample. If glucose is < 60 mg / dL
or > 300 mg / dL, refer to Pediatric Hypoglycemia or
Hyperglycemia Patient Care Protocol.
Establish an IV of normal saline at KVO. Titrate to a
systolic pressure appropriate for child:
a.
Birth to 1 month - 60 mmHg
b.
1 month to 1 year - > 70 mmHg
c.
Greater than 1 year - 70 + [2 x Age (years)]
If hypoperfusion is suspected, refer to the Pediatric
Shock protocol.
Perform ongoing assessment as indicated and transport
promptly.
