Nausea and Vomitting
protocolid=80
==============================
EMTP/EMTI
-----------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%
<tr><td>4.<td>Allow the patient to lie in a comfortable position.
<tr><td>5.<td>Establish an IV of normal saline per patient assessment.
<tr><td>6.<td>Assess for signs of shock. If shock is suspected, follow the <i><u>Pediatric Shock protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>7.<td>For severe nausea or vomiting, if available, give <i><u>ONDANSETRON (ZOFRAN)</u></i>.
â€¢ *If only IV formulation is available, administer 0.1 mg / kg IV / IM up to 4 mg over 2 to 5 minutes.*
<tr><td>8.<td>May repeat Ondansetron PO or IV dosing after 10 minutes if needed.
<tr><td>9.<td>Perform ongoing assessment as indicated and transport.
