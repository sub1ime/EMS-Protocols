Perform general patient management.
Support life-threatening problems associated with
airway, breathing, and circulation.
Assess for signs of trauma. Provide spinal
immobilization as necessary.
Administer oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
For altered mental status, perform rapid glucose
determination.
If glucose > 300 mg / dL, start an IV of normal saline.
For signs and symptoms of hypovolemic shock or
dehydration, follow the Pediatric Shock protocol.

10.

If glucose level is > 300 mg / dL, and no signs of
shock are noted, administer maintenance Normal
Saline infusion:
•
4.0 ml / kg for first 1 - 10 kg of weight.
•
Add 2.0 ml / kg for next 11 - 20 kg of weight.
•
Add 1.0 ml / kg, for every kg of weight, > 20 kg.
•
Multiply total amount x 2= total hourly
hyperglycemic maintenance amount.
Place on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret 12
lead ECG per assessment.
Transport and perform ongoing assessment as
indicated.
