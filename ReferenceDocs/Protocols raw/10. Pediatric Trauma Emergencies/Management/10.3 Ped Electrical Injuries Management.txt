Ped Electrical Injuries
protocolid=90
==================
EMTP/EMTI
-------------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Consider supporting respirations with a BVM.
<tr><td>4.<td>Determine extent of any burn injuries. Refer to the <i><u>Pediatric Burns protocol</u></i>. Avoid initiating IVs in areas of burn unless absolutely necessary.
<tr><td>5.<td> patient on cardiac monitor; obtain / interpret <i><u>12 Lead ECG</u></i>. Refer to the appropriate <i><u>Pediatric Cardiac Care protocol</u></i> for dysrhythmias.
<tr><td>6.<td> an IV of normal saline to titrate an appropriate BP:
<tr><td>a.<td> to 1 month - > 60 mmHg
<tr><td>b.<td>1 month to 1 year - > 70 mmHg
<tr><td>c.<td>Greater than 1 year - 70 + [2 x Age (years)]
<tr><td>7.<td> administration of pain management per <i><u>Pediatric Pain Management protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Transport to an appropriate facility and perform ongoing assessment as indicated.
