Drowning and Near Drowning Management
protocolid=49
=====================================
EMTP/EMTI
-----------------
<table><tr><td>1.<td>Perform general patient management.
<tr><td>2.<td>Support life-threatening problems associated with airway, breathing, and circulation. Assess mechanism or injury and C-spine precautions.
<tr><td>3.<td>Administer oxygen to maintain <i><u>SPO</u></i><sub>2</sub> 94 - 99%. Consider supporting respirations with a BVM.
<tr><td>4.<td>If the patient is in critical respiratory distress, consider placement of <b>orotracheal</b> intubation.
<tr><td>5.<td>Remove wet clothing and prevent heat loss. If suspected, refer to <i><u>Hypothermia protocol</u></i>.
<tr><td>6.<td>Monitor <i><u>capnography</u></i>.
<tr><td>7.<td>Place patient on cardiac monitor and obtain / interpret <i><u>12 lead ECG</u></i>.
<tr><td>8.<td>Establish an IV of normal saline at KVO.
<tr><td>9.<td>Transport and perform ongoing assessment as indicated.
