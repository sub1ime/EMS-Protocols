Tricyclic Overdose

===============
Obtain general assessment of the patient.
Administer Oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
Suction oropharynx as necessary.
Obtain blood glucose sample. If glucose is < 60 mg / dL
or > 300 mg / dL, refer to Hypoglycemia or
Hyperglycemia protocol.
Establish IV of Normal Saline. Titrate to maintain a
systolic BP > 90 mmHg.
Place the patient on a cardiac monitor and obtain /
interpret 12 lead ECG.
If TCA overdose is suspected and any progressive
widening of QRS, >.12 ms, seizure activity, hypotension,
tachycardia or heart block is noted, administer SODIUM
BICARBONATE 50 mEq IVP.
Transport promptly in position of comfort. Reassess vital
signs as indicated.
