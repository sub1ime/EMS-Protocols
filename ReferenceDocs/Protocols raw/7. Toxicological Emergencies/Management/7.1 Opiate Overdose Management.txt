Obtain general patient assessment.
Administer Oxygen to maintain SPO 2 94 - 99%
Suction oropharynx as necessary.
Obtain blood glucose sample. If glucose is < 60 mg / dL
or > 300 mg / dL, refer to the Hypoglycemia or
Hyperglycemia Protocol.
If necessary, refer to Patient Restraint protocol.
Place patient on cardiac monitor.
Establish IV of Normal Saline. Titrate rate to maintain
systolic BP > 90 mmHg.
For suspected opiate overdose, administer NALOXONE,
0.4 - 2.0 mg slow IVP to maintain an adequate
respiratory effort. Dose may be repeated as necessary.
Transport promptly in position of comfort. Reassess VS
as indicated.
